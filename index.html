Features

Everything one broker needs to run like a desk of ten.

Built with brokers in the loop. If we wouldn't use it ourselves, it doesn't ship.

Multi-market sync

One listing across StubHub, SeatGeek, Vivid Seats, Lysted, Ticombo and more — a sale anywhere pulls it everywhere else in under 5 seconds.

AutoPrice engine

Live comps every minute, your floor and ceiling rules applied, every channel repriced automatically.

True P&L per seat

Cost basis, fees, shipping and refunds tracked per seat — know your margin before the show, not weeks after.

Speculation tracking

Spec a section before tickets drop; Stockwise reconciles the position the moment the seats land.

Multi-currency, multi-region

Buy, sell and settle in different currencies, tracked at FX-adjusted cost. USD, EUR, GBP, AED.
